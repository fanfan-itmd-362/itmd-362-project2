IITALK

How do we spread information in this modern day world?

IITALK is an organization in which people will be able to freely express, display, and demonstrate amazing finding and ideas with tons of people from around the world that come to IIT. Sole purpose is to spread ideas and concepts through powerful talks given by all types of people. Topics will go from mathematical theories, biomedical discoveries to new technological advances. All these events will help share ideas around the world in the IIT community.
